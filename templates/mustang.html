1967 Ford Mustang

Overview: Known as the quintessential American muscle car, the 1967 Ford Mustang brought a new level of power and performance to the Mustang lineup. It featured a more aggressive design with a larger grille and side scoops.

Engine: 289 cubic inch V8 engine, producing up to 271 horsepower.

Top Speed: Approximately 120 mph (193 km/h).

Key Features: Unique design with fastback body style, high-performance V8 options, and a powerful reputation among muscle cars.
