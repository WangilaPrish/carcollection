1973 Porsche 911

Overview: The 1973 Porsche 911 introduced the world to the Carrera RS, an iconic sports car known for its lightweight construction and rear-engine layout. It quickly became a favorite for both street and track enthusiasts.

Engine: 2.7-liter flat-six engine, producing around 210 horsepower.

Top Speed: Approximately 150 mph (241 km/h).

Key Features: Lightweight design, distinctive ducktail spoiler, and legendary handling characteristics that cemented Porsche's reputation.
